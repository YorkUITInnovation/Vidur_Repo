Who We Are

About the IT Innovation Team

The IT Innovation Team at York University is a dedicated group of technologists, developers, and strategists committed to advancing the university's digital landscape.

We partner with faculties, departments, and students to identify challenges and architect scalable, modern solutions — from enterprise platforms to bespoke applications.
